# SparkP2P Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 1 April 2026
**Last Updated:** 1 April 2026

## 1. Introduction

SparkP2P ("we", "us", "our") is a peer-to-peer cryptocurrency trading platform operated in Kenya. We are committed to protecting the personal data of our users in accordance with the **Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 (DPA)** and any applicable regulations issued by the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC).

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

## 3. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal data:

### 3.1 Identity Data
- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Security question and answer (for account verification)

### 3.2 Financial Data
- M-Pesa phone number
- Bank paybill number and account number
- Transaction amounts and references
- Wallet balances

### 3.3 Technical Data
- IP address (via server logs)
- Login timestamps
- Device/browser information (via standard HTTP headers)

### 3.4 Third-Party Integration Data
- Binance account credentials (encrypted cookies and session tokens) — stored solely to execute trades on behalf of the user
- Binance UID and username

### 3.5 Usage Data
- Trade history (order IDs, amounts, counterparties)
- Platform activity logs

## 4. Why We Collect Your Data (Legal Basis)

| Purpose | Data Used | Legal Basis |
|---|---|---|
| Account registration and identity verification | Name, email, phone | Contractual necessity |
| Processing trades and settlements | Financial data, Binance credentials | Contractual necessity |
| Fraud prevention and account security | Login logs, failed attempts, lockouts | Legitimate interest |
| Regulatory compliance and AML/KYC | Identity and transaction data | Legal obligation |
| Customer support | Name, email, transaction history | Legitimate interest |
| Platform improvement and analytics | Aggregated usage data | Legitimate interest |
| Sending transactional SMS/email notifications | Phone, email | Contractual necessity |

We do **not** use your data for marketing without your explicit consent.

---

## 5. Secondary Use of Customer Data

We are committed to the principle of **purpose limitation**. Your personal data will only be used for the specific purposes stated at the time of collection.

**We will NOT:**
- Sell your personal data to any third party
- Share your data with advertisers or data brokers
- Use your data to build profiles for sale or transfer
- Use your data for any purpose unrelated to providing the SparkP2P service without your explicit consent

**We MAY share your data with:**
- **Safaricom / M-Pesa** — to process payment settlements (necessary to fulfil the service)
- **Africa's Talking** — to deliver SMS OTP and notification messages
- **Brevo (Sendinblue)** — to deliver transactional emails
- **Binance** — your credentials are submitted to Binance's platform on your behalf to execute trades you have authorized
- **Regulatory authorities** — where required by law (e.g., Financial Reporting Centre, DCI, ODPC)

Any third-party processors we engage are bound by data processing agreements and are prohibited from using your data for their own purposes.

---

## 6. Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored on servers located within a reputable cloud provider (DigitalOcean)
- Sensitive credentials (Binance cookies, 2FA secrets, fund passwords) are **encrypted at rest**
- Passwords are stored as **bcrypt hashes** — never in plain text
- Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel only
- All data in transit is protected by **TLS 1.2+** (HTTPS)
- Administrative access requires authentication and is logged

## 8. Your Rights Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019

You have the right to:

1. **Access** — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
2. **Rectification** — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
3. **Erasure** — Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention obligations)
4. **Restriction** — Request that we limit processing of your data
5. **Portability** — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
6. **Objection** — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
7. **Withdraw Consent** — Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at: **support@sparkp2p.com**

We will respond within **21 days** as required by the DPA.
